Detailed Description

template<typename... T>
class lanxc::promise< T >

The promise for a future.

A promise guards a process that a future is getting delivered. When a promise is destructed the corresponding future is either fulfilled if set_result has been called or cancelled. The reason that a future get cancelled is a instance of #invalid_future, or other value provided by set_exception_ptr or set_exception and can be caught as a std::exception_ptr by the future.
